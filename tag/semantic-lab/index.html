Linked Jazz Now Part of Semantic Lab at Pratt

We are pleased to announce the introduction of the Semantic Lab at Pratt, which will serve as a testbed and incubator for the development of novel methodologies and tools for the application of semantic technologies to libraries, archives, and museums. Linked Jazz will now find its new home under The Semantic Lab collective, along with new projects, including DADAlytics. We appreciate your continued interest and support as we expand the Linked Jazz semantic work in new directions.
