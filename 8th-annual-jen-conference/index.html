8th Annual JEN Conference

In an effort to tackle the gender bias on Wikipedia and foster jazz appreciation, Linked Jazz hosted a New Orleans Women of Jazz Wikipedia edit-a-thon at the 8th Annual JEN Conference this January.   Search #WomenofJazz on Twitter to see what fellow Wikipedians contributed!

New Orleans Women of Jazz Wikipedia Edit-A-Thon live at the 9th Annual JEN Conference in New Orleans on January 6, 2017.
